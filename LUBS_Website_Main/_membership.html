Membership

Membership Types

To apply, simply fill out the required information. The Hong Kong Blockchain Society will receive and process your application within 5-8 business days. If accepted, the successful applicant would receive a offer letter for membership and details for membership fee payment. All memberships are valid for 365 days starting from the moment that the membership application has been approved. Please contact us by email or facebook for enquiries.

    • Student

    • Community Member

      • Eligibility for persons actively working in or seeking to contribute to the blockchain industry who are enrolled in good standing with a recognised educational institution

      • Eligible to vote: no

      • Course Discount: 50%

      • Access to Library: yes

      • Access to Job Board: yes

      • Exclusive Events: no

      • May sponsor Events: no

      • May request Events: no

    • Apply

    • Professional

    • Community Member

      • Eligible for persons actively working in or seeking to contribute to the blockchain industry who are engaged in or seeking part- or full-time employment

      • Eligible to vote: no

      • Course Discount: 0%

      • Access to Library: yes

      • Access to Job Board: yes

      • Exclusive Events: no

      • May sponsor Events: no

      • May request Events: no

    • Apply

    • Corporation

    • Corporate Member

      • Eligible for organisations with valid business registration from a recognised legal jurisdiction that are interested in or already involved in the blockchain industry

      • Eligible to vote: no

      • Course Discount: 20%

      • Access to Library: yes

      • Access to Job Board: yes

      • Exclusive Events: yes

      • May sponsor Events: yes

      • May request Events: yes

    • Apply

    • Non-Committee

    • Service Member

      • Eligible for persons with a valid work permit or volunteer visa or equivalent for the relevant jurisdiction who contribute work and resources to The Society

      • Eligible to vote: yes

      • Course Discount: 100%

      • Access to Library: yes

      • Access to Job Board: yes

      • Exclusive Events: yes

      • May sponsor Events: yes

      • May request Events: no
