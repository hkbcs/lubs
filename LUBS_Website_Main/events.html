Access to Justice Hackathon: Belt Road Justice Challenge 2018 (Hong Kong Law Society)

The Access to Justice InnoTech Law Hackathon (“A2J Hackathon”) organised by the ITC, which is the first legal hackathon in Asia organised by a legal professional body and the first hackathon in Asia with an “access to justice” theme, was successfully held in April this year. Over 120 legal professionals, I.T. experts and students gathered together at this event to amalgamate their expertise, knowledge and skills to design technology-enabled solutions to address different access to justice issues.
