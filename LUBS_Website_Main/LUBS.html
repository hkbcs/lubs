LUBS Activities

Blockchain Training for University Students

The Lingnan University Blockchain Society is the strategic partner of the HKBCS providing educational resources for LU students. We are planning to organize Lingnan University Blockchain Olympiad or LUBCOL in Hong Kong, supported by the Office Of Student Affairs (OSA) and funded by LEI(Lingnan Entrepreneurship Initiative). → Learn More

Blockchain Community For Startup Development

We provide talks and events from our industrial partners on the relevance of Blockchain applications. Our vast network of startups, VCs and sponsors is fully accessible for members through the Society. LEI or Lingnan Entrepreneurship Initiative, and Postgraduate Degree Associates will help you to consider practical applications of blockchain in business analytics, healthcare, geographical information systems etc. We have wide range of professionals who will help you to understand blockchain better through our free meetings with university professors. Check the latest university research outputs related to blockchain on LU Scholars! → Learn More

Career opportunities through our sponsors and partners.

The Lingnan University Blockchain Society provides future career consultations and helps to connect people with industry professinals and enterprises. Lingnan Career Advising Services of OSA, and Lingnan Entrepreneurship Initiative will assist future students in our community to make informed career choices commensurate with their talents, interests and aptitude in preparation for employment after graduation. Career education and development programmes are organized to guide students through different phases of the career planning stage. Recruitment talks and placement activities are held throughout the year to provide the latest job market information and channels for direct interaction with employers. → Learn More
