With The Real World

We work with government, academia, and industry to promote legitimate blockchain development through education, research, and other activities and services in Hong Kong and the surrounding region.

Academic Activities

Blockchain/Regtech Training for University Students

The Hong Kong Blockchain Society is the strategic partner of the City University of Hong Kong for the International Blockchain Olympiad or IBCOL in Hong Kong, supported by the Office of the Government Chief Information Officer (OGCIO) and funded by HSBC. → Learn More

Blockchain Training for Secondary School Students

The Hong Kong Blockchain Society has launched The Five Levels of ABCD or ABC5-Lab, a STEM education programme that prepares Hong Kong's secondary school students for enriched IT learning experience and competitions supported by the Office of the Government Chief Information Officer (OGCIO). → Learn More

Societal Trends

Combating COVID-19

The Hong Kong Blockchain Society is working with its members, stakeholders, and startups on various initiatives to fight against the global 2019 Coronavirus Disease pandemic, from digital immunity passports to tracking hardware provenance. → Learn More

Blockchain as a National Priority

In October 2019, Chinese President Xi Jinping emphasized the use of blockchain as an important breakthrough for independent innovation of core technologies, and urged to accelerate the development of blockchain technology and industrial innovation. → Learn More
