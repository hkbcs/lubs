About

AGENDA

Blockchain for University Students

We are a student organization at LU focused on creating meaningful conversations and collaborations around trust-minimizing distributed systems. In our weekly reading and discussion group, we cover everything from the Inter-Planetary File System to the latest advances on zero-knowledge proofs to Ethereum's new scalability solutions. We also host a few big events every year as part of the broader blockchain community. We are in charge of organization of Lingnan University Blockchain Olympiad 2022 or LUBCOL 2022 in Hong Kong, supported by the Office Of Student Affairs (OSA) and funded by LEI(Lingnan Entrepreneurship Initiative). → Learn More

MEMBERSHIP

Our Journey Towards Blockchain

Membership is important due to the nature of the events we aim to deliver on a weekly basis. Since they are likely to attract sizeable crowds from LU students (but also other universities and young professionals), membership fees goes towards running them as efficiently as possible, with refreshments provided. Events such as "Decentralised Drinks" and pizza evenings are also common and provided to members as a way to socialise and network with other blockchain enthusiasts, giving everyone a chance to meet new friends. Having been awarded a "Bronze" star this year, we are committed to giving back to the LU community, running a mentorship program and developing close ties with firms in the industry such as Consensys, Vega protocol and Outlier Ventures, all offering expert advice for employment/internship opportunities. We also offer members the chance to run for the committee in Hong Kong Blockchain Society, the HK's largest hub for Blockchaih, either in the labs, market, events or marketing team, giving them a fantastic opportunity to oversee the running of a world-class student community with the principal aim of educating others on the novel technology. → Learn More
